Privacy Policy — Puzzle Hub

Last updated: 9 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Puzzle Hub (“the app”, “we”, “us”) handles information when you use it. Puzzle Hub is developed by Piktus.

Summary

Puzzle Hub is a single-player daily-puzzle game. We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, email address, contacts, photos, precise location, or any content you type. The only data we handle is anonymous, aggregated gameplay analytics used to understand how the game is played and to improve it — and you can turn that off at any time from within the app.

Information we collect

Gameplay analytics (via Google Firebase Analytics)

To understand how players use the game, we use Google Firebase Analytics. It records anonymous, event-level gameplay data, such as:

  • Which puzzles are opened, solved, or abandoned, and how long that took
  • Progression signals grouped into ranges (e.g. level “10–19”, streak “7–13”, coin balance “500–1999”) — never exact personal profiles
  • In-game economy events (coins earned/spent, hints used, daily spins)
  • Which screens are viewed and whether the tutorial was completed

This data is not tied to your real-world identity. It is used only in aggregate to see what is fun, what is confusing, and what to fix.

Data collected automatically by Firebase

When analytics is enabled, Google Firebase automatically collects standard technical information required to operate the analytics service, including:

  • A randomly generated app-instance identifier and device/advertising identifiers
  • Device model, operating-system version, and app version
  • Approximate (coarse, country/region-level) location derived from IP address
  • General app-usage and diagnostic information

Google processes this data as described in Google’s Privacy Policy and How Google uses data when you use partners’ sites or apps.

What we do NOT collect

  • No account, sign-in, name, or email address
  • No precise/GPS location
  • No contacts, photos, files, microphone, or camera data
  • No text you enter into puzzles
  • We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with advertising networks (the app currently contains no third-party ads).

Your choices

  • Turn analytics off. Puzzle Hub includes an in-app toggle that stops all analytics collection. When you switch it off, no further events are sent.
  • Reset the app. Clearing the app’s data or uninstalling it removes locally stored game progress from your device.

Data storage and retention

Your game progress (levels, coins, streaks, solved puzzles) is stored locally on your device, not on our servers. Anonymous analytics data is retained by Google Firebase according to the retention period configured in our Firebase project and Google’s standard policies.

Children’s privacy

Puzzle Hub is a general-audience puzzle game. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because we collect no personal identifiers, the app does not build advertising or behavioural profiles of any user, including children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
